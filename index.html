About the website

The Bhagavad Gita, also known as the "Song of the Lord," is a sacred scripture that is a part of the Mahabharata, one of the two major Sanskrit epics of India. It is a conversation between the prince Arjuna and his guide and charioteer, Lord Krishna, who is revealed to be an avatar (incarnation) of Lord Vishnu.

The Bhagavad Gita consists of 18 chapters and a total of 700 verses, and it is considered to be one of the most important texts in Indian philosophy and religious thought. It is revered for its profound insights into the nature of the self, the purpose of life, and the path to enlightenment.

The Bhagavad Gita teaches that true happiness and fulfillment come from living a life of dharma, or righteous duty, and from developing a personal relationship with God through devotion and selfless action. It also emphasizes the importance of yoga and meditation as means of attaining inner peace and clarity of mind.

Bhagavad Gita is the best thing to read to strengthen our knowledge and to attain real spiritual peace and happiness. With this e-book of Bhagavad Gita we aim to make the book accessible and provide a convenient and sustainable way for people to engage with the Bhagavad Gita.
